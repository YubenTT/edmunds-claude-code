# Comprehensive UO Server Research Report
## Best Global Ultima Online Servers as Alternatives to UO Outlands

**Research Date:** November 16, 2025
**Focus:** Active global UO servers/shards for international players

---

## Executive Summary

UO Outlands remains the most populated UO freeshard with **3,000+ concurrent players** during peak times and **1,000-1,500 average concurrent players**. However, several excellent alternatives exist for players seeking different gameplay styles, from classic Renaissance-era experiences to PvE-focused communities.

This report identifies **7 top-tier alternatives** with active populations, established communities, and unique features that differentiate them from Outlands.

## Safety & Security Recommendations

Based on community discussions:

1. **Always use unique usernames and passwords for each server**
   - Some defunct server admins have attempted to use credentials on other servers
   - Never reuse Outlands credentials elsewhere

2. **Stick to established servers**
   - Servers running 3+ years are generally trustworthy
   - Check community feedback and history

3. **Avoid sharing account info**
   - No legitimate server will ask for your password
   - Be wary of "admin" messages asking for credentials

4. **Check server reputation**
   - Look for Discord communities
   - Read recent forum posts
   - Verify server is actively maintained

## Conclusion

While UO Outlands dominates the freeshard landscape with its population and innovation, excellent alternatives exist for different playstyles:

- **UO Evolution** offers the closest match in content volume with a PvE focus
- **UO Renaissance** provides the authentic classic experience
- **UOAlive** delivers the safest, most welcoming PvE environment
- **UO Forever** maintains a strong PvP community with classic mechanics
- **UORPG** innovates with unique systems for players wanting something different

The UO freeshard community remains vibrant in 2024-2025, with active development, dedicated communities, and diverse options for every playstyle. The key is identifying what aspects of UO matter most to you and choosing a server that aligns with those priorities.

**No single server can replace Outlands' combination of population, content, and polish** - but these alternatives each excel in specific areas and provide compelling reasons to try them.
